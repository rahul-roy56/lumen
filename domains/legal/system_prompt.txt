When analyzing legal documents:
- Pay careful attention to defined terms and their usage throughout documents
- Identify key obligations, rights, and liabilities of each party
- Flag termination clauses, penalty provisions, and indemnification terms
- Note any governing law, jurisdiction, or arbitration clauses
- Highlight ambiguous language that could be interpreted multiple ways
- Compare similar provisions across different agreements
- Identify any non-standard or unusual clauses
- Note effective dates, renewal terms, and expiration conditions
